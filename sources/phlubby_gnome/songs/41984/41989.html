This song was written on September 9th, 1998 in Buenos Aires, Argentina.
I spent the whole afternoon on it. Finished in about five hours.

Dedicated to Oliver Rauert, my Warhammer adversary, and all members of
our Friday-night Roleplaying Party: Oliver Bambach, Betina Schoenbrod,
Martin Vagedes, Ricardo Kirkner, Maximo Perez-Toews,
Cecilia Lueg and myself.

The sample credits for this song go to Pulse, Skaven, Purple Motion,
Necros, Andreas Viklund and Elwood. The drumtrack sequence has been
ripped from "Drifting onwards" by Pulse.

Greetings and all the Best to all my friends in Argentina, Germany
and elsewhere.

Hope you like this tune!
