"Beacon"
Written by Novus
Released for Sound Devotion
16-bit version released on Thursday, September 30th, 1999.
8-bit version released on Sunday, March 19th, 2000.

For this 8-Bit Mix of "Beacon," I mixed all of the 16-bit samples down to
8-bit and then cut out the whispered vocals and the sound effects. I'm not
nearly as happy with this version of "Beacon" as I am with the
full-quality 16-bit version, but a lot of people were balking at
downloading a 2-meg file, and I realized I had to be practical.

I will say this, though... although very few people have downloaded the
16-bit version of "Beacon," none of them have regretted it. So, if you
enjoyed this mix, you ain't heard nothing yet. Go to my web page and get
the 16-bit version. You won't be sorry.
