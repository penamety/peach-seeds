New stuff.
Pure ZX Spectrum 48K with its powerful BEEPER, that is:

The Dynamic Duo ;) of ZX48 chips -

**Zilog Z80** and **Ferranti ULA** -
harnessed again, to generate more 1-bit music with strange new synthesis in 9 channels of sound (8 tones + drums).

I composed this song using a non-published (yet) engine programmed by Retrospec, based on the old good Octode.
Except the new way of synthesis, there are also introduced 2 levels of volume (4 loud and 4 quieter channels).
Hence - more space, more weight, in Beeper music.
Recorded from real hardware.

Compiled data (TAP file) is only 8Kb big (which is small for the Octode and its clones).
