Additional Information:

This song is based on the title music of the movie of the same name ("Uuno Turhapuro goes to countryside").

The original melody is probably written by Jaakko Salo (1930-2002).

The Finnish low budget comedy movie (shot in 20 days) tells a story about notorious guy Uuno Turhapuro who this time moves to the countryside where his father produces illicit booze.
