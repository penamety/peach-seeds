Additional Information:

This is *NOT* AY! I used this category only because there's no corner for Spectrum's Z80 chip sound.

ZX Spectrum 48K chiptune (Z80 chip).

This is a chiptune with sound-quality of ONE BIT (sound turn-on, sound turn-off, nothing more). Generated by Z80 chip with ULA chip as a transducer.

This editor (Orfeus) makes from original hardware one channel of square wave without any volume control - 3 channels, in a way of software mixing, adding envelopes and simulating volume's changes.

However, like they wrote on 8BC, this is a tune for TWO beepers - for two synchronized Spectrums48. :)

Which gives 6 channels.
