This is the soundtrack to "Anaconda", one of the three cell-phone styled minigames hidden in the Free Radical Design release TimeSplitters 2 (PS2/GC/XBox). Graeme Norgate composed a unique 8-bit soundtrack for each. Players unlock the minigame by finding the hidden game cartridge in the level, then accessing it through the character's in-game communicator tool.
