Written on or about Aug/93. (c)
This is my first experience with Ultra Tracker, and I like it. It could really use a JUMP effect, though...
This song was based on an old idea I had for a song, years ago. I hope you enjoy the song as much as I enjoyed composing it.

I've heard 4 track MODfiles that blow the hell out of most popular music on the market these days, and the artists above are probably THE best in the tracker-composing field, and are using 8 to 32 tracks. I've just run out of writing space (32 lines). Adieu... Killer Fish
