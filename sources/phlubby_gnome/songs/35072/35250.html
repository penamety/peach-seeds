MAS_UTrack_V003 The Lair Of The Orange Machine By: Killer Fish (Todd Showalter) of Late Nite Software Labs
--------------------------------
Written on or about Mar/94. (c)
This song took insanely long to write, especially considering that it is rather small. It did spend a lot of time on the back burner waiting for UltraTracker to get a Jump command. I hope one comes out soon, but I've decided that this song was a pot that I'd been staring at far too long. If I finish it, perhaps it will boil, and UltraTracker will get a Jump command. It's a luck thing I'm trying here, so don't break the mojo.
Anyways, as I was blathering above, this song's been about in my "to be completed" directory for a while. I believe that it dates back to the days of the original UltraTracker 1.0 (most of my sample archive has since become UWF files).
I hope you enjoy the song. I'd like to continue to fill your life with my vast wisdom, but I've just run out of space.
Gotta speed, keid...
Killer Fish
