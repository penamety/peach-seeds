MAS_UTrack_V003 No Time! By: Killer Fish (Todd Showalter) of Late Nite Software Labs
--------------------------------
Written on or about Mar/94. (c)
This song was written as a response to about 12 deadlines, all of which descended on me from out of the clear blue sky. I spent a week of frantic work to get everything done, and when I had nothing left that needed to be done, this song is how I burned off the rest of my vast supply of adrenalin before I crawled into bed and fell into a deep, healing sleep.
As usual, I have managed to use a 32 voice tracker to create a song that uses less than 10 channels. I'm working on wider music files (trust me...) but they won't be released until I can listen to them without openly cringing. May take a while...
