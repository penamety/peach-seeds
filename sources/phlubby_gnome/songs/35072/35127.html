Additional Information:

Orchestral tune inspired by music from the movie "The Rock" composed by Harry Gregson-Williams, Nick-Glennie Smith and Hans Zimmer.

I think this tune has a great movie-atmosphere, consisting of different parts trying to portray different moods/settings. I tried to visualize different happenings in a movie while composing this song.

Most samples are "borrowed" from the Lizardking song "Age of Legends".
