SACRIFICE THE RAPPER

Written, composed and recorded by Malekith in May 2000

For long I had wanted to make a joke-song, and when I got my new ZOOM
GFX-707 guitar effect processor, I thought it would be an excellent
occasion to test it :))
So I found the idea and wrote stupid lyrics during a philosophy class :-)
Then, I composed this bloody shit in one hour, played and recorded it (I
used a microphone plugged into my SB Live 128 soundcard). Finally one of
my friends growled the verses, and I tried to howl the chorus :))

I hope you've understood this is not serious at all :)
So have fun with that shit !
Inspiration got from Children Of Bodom and some Cadaver stuff :)

Hails : Cadaver, Dragon, Hakan, HolyBlood, Darkium, Razor, ChristCrusha,
Neomancia, and everyone who likes this :)
