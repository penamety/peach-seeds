Additional Information:

--------------------

Tracked by Malekith for the Metal Madness musicdisk, available at
http://www.trackedaggression.com
All guitars and backing vocals were recorded by Malekith in September
2002. Lead vocals were sung by BHS on 29/09/2002.

Originally by Running Wild, available on their "Gates To Purgatory" debut
album (1984) and on the "First Years Of Piracy" collection of
re-recordings (1991). This cover is actually based upon this version,
because it has much more energy than the original (heavier sound, faster
chorus, better backing vocals etc...)

Something quite amusing : read the lyrics, you'll notice that the words on
RW's two first albums had nothing to do with their famous pirate-stuff...
Instead, there's true excellent evil lyrics :) and I think the contrast
with the rock-sounding riffs is quite funny :)

This is certainly one of my favourite Running Wild songs, and has one of
my favorite choruses. It's just too bad they don't play it live anymore...
maybe it's because this song wasn't written by their mastermind
Rock'n'Rolf, but by their former second guitarist who left a couple of
years later, Preacher ? Singing that chorus at a show must have been truly
amazing, I wish I were one of the old farts who heard that :D
