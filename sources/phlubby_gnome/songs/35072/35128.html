Additional Information:

Second part of 'The Assault'. Also inspired by music from the movie "The Rock" composed by Harry Gregson-Williams, Nick-Glennie Smith and Hans Zimmer.

I've also tried to capture the movie atmosphere in this song, and it is divided into seperate parts attempting to portray different scenarios in a movie. This song is in my opinion better technically performed than the "First Stage" (stereo effects and so on).

Most samples are identical to the ones in 'The Assault - First Stage', and are "borrowed" from the Lizardking song "Age of Legends".
