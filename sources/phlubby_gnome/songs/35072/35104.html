WRATH OF THE DEAD - 3'54

Written, composed and tracked by Malekith
Vocals by Cadaver
Samples by Betrayer, Cadaver and some others... thanx a lot

This song was supposed to be released for the true-metal mod group Iron
Assault, but since i've had no news of the organizer Deimos since July, I
decided to release it while making my website. So here it is. The song was
written in November '98 and has been constantly improved and updated until
now.

I'd like to say a big HI to all my friends from the scene, and to thank
all those who helped me by sending feedback after the release of TA4 and
"Ahead On The Way". Special thanks go to Cadaver for making these great
vocals !

The lyrics are in the text file that is normally included in the zip.

...And if you have something to say, go and contact me before the mad
souls of the dead damn you forevermore, you poor mortal !
