PLAIN OF BONES

Written, arranged and tracked by Malekith
Released on 29/04/99 (my first released original tune)
Redone during December '99 for my new website (in fact, I just checked the
guitar volumes, modified a little bit the drum track and added some
brass and strings...)

This tune was inspired mainly by Edguy and Rhapsody, and also Maiden for
the harmonized guitar solos :)

Thanks to Betrayer for guitar samples and to everyone who helped me.
Hails to Dragon, Hakan, Cadaver, Gnome, and all the Metalscene
members and all those who support metal tracking.
And of course you, if you like this !

Playing time : 6'34
