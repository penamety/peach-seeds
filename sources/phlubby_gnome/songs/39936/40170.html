«Buratino» Strikes Back ;) ...of course, on AY!

The original song is called "Bu-ra-ti-no! The final song" (Buratino - it's a Russian Pinocchio). Music by Alexey Rybnikov, lyrics by Yuri Entin.
It comes from the Soviet children's musical film "The Adventures of Buratino" (on russian "Приключения Буратино"), Belarusfilm, USSR, 1975.
Based on Aleksey Nikolayevich Tolstoy's tale "The Golden Key, or Adventures of Buratino", USSR, 1936.
