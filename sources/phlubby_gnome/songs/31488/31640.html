Additional Information:

This is a complete revision of the One Must Fall 2097 theme song from 1994. Feeling nostalgic one day, I went on Youtube and searched for One Must Fall. To my surprise, I've found a lot of positive comments and remixes to my OMF2097 songs. That was when I decided to 'reconstruct' the theme song as a weekend project for everyone. Enjoy!
