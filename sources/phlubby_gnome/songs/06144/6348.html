About the samples... "The main sources where ripped samples from Chris Huelsbeck’s R-Type track,drum and speech samples from several tracks of the album ‘Romeo Knight’ (yes,that’s where my handle originates from!) of the not-so-well-known Hip-Hop band Boogie Boys (except the ‘Red Sector’-sample, that’s my own voice) and I can get even more specific: The “dommm” sample was taken from Art of Noise’s “Closeto the Edge” and the wobbly bass came from Erasure’s “Say What” (on the album “Wonderland” from 1985)."
