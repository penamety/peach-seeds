http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iCdc0ATVgLQ

The demo featured a SoundTracker playroutine written in ARM assembler by Hugo Fiennes which was the first to be written on the Archimedes platform.

Many years later Hugo went on to head the iPhone 4 hardware design team at Apple!!
