Additional Information:

Timbral was playing a cool oldschool
live set at Evoke 2010. I liked it quite a bit, so I had to go and download
some more of his music from the net.
A 2010 live set of his had
this strangely named, fun and catchy tune that started playing in
my head every now and then.

When you play a chiptune in your head, at some point
your imagination kind of starts adding elements to it, breaking
the technical limitations of the original chip. (Or that's
what happens to me anyway.)
Eventually I decided I might as
well compose this remix in FL Studio and play it from the speakers
instead of just the brain. Here you go.
