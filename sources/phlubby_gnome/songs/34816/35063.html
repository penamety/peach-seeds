Additional Information:

Ukko Nooa (in swedish Gubben Noak) is a famous traditional song about Noah from the Bible.

It is a children's song but in Sweden it is used also as a drinking song. Finnish composer Oskar Merikanto has composed variations for piano based on this theme.
