Additional Information:

This is the first of two tracks that I've composed for this excellent Scenesat release.
Originally, the tune was supposed to be accompanied by a demo. However, the demo wasn't finished so I decided to release it here as a standalone track. It's a pretty straightforward demo tune that was supposed to go along with crazy camera flying and a generally hard and tekky atmosphere. Repetitiveness for FTW!
