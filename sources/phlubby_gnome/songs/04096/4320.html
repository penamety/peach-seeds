Listening instructions:

Imagine yourself standing on the field with an amazing stormfront
coming up right next to you. You are taken away by it's power, whirled
around and then you are able to reach the top of the clouds. Gazing at th
e sun you approach the blue sky...

Or something like that. I know I've got nothing else to do than writing
you some stuff like that above. But I looked at that message-window and it
was so empty and lonely and... but enough of that.

This tune is the result of the boring Saturday mornings, when I get up,
still curing the coma of the last night and the only thing I've got to do
is to abuse my computer for tracking music. After the last two
"orchestra"-XMs, I thought the time has come again to make something
primitive again. So I took my worst and biggest piano-sample, a really
bad
basedrum and a simple beep-sample and here it is, my newest dance-track.
Or whatever you would call that style. Well, finally composing this piece
of music took me about one day hard and less hard work.

I hope you enjoy it and we'll see what's next.

I promise: the next one will be an XM again. (less channels and you don't
have to spend such a lot of time in reading this damn messages.)
