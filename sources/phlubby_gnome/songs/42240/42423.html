Additional Information:

"Porilaisten marssi" (engl. "The march of Pori's regiment", swedish: "Björneborgarnas marsch") is a Finnish military march. It is used by both Finnish and Estonian armies. It is also commonly heard during the appearance of the president and sport broadcasts. The composer is unknown. Song's origins go back to the 18th century.
