AMIGA 500 (8 channels).
Made with OctaMED.
Requires 1Mb of ChipRam.
Recorded from real hardware. (Stereo slightly changed).

Yesssssssss. After 20 years I made an 8-channels module on Amiga500 AGAIN. ;) I wasn't sure how it would work, as once can't actually use volumes in this mode, on OctaMED. It's because there is only 4 volumes for 8 traxx then. Which suxx. So it's better not to use volumes at all and make more 'version' of samples, with various volumes.
Also the sound-quality gets much lower in the 8ch mode. But whatta hell - why not to force old good A500 to some harder work (software mixing: actually two chips play the music: Paula but also Motorola :) ).

Enjoy this small experiment. ;)
