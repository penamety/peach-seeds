just a quick informal info file..

wow, two years! march 1995 -> march 1997 .. and still going. bear
with us here, i dont think we had any idea the group would last this
long. =)

we would just like to thank everyone who has supported our music over
the group's inception (and before when we were running around in other
various groups trying to be heard.. =)).. and all your mail to the
list *is read*, even if not replied to and we appreciate it all..

we don't know how much longer the group will be together (no, we don't
SEE an end in sight but we are all becoming increasingly busy with our
lives outside the computer so who knows what the future will bring.)

that's about it, bye for now, all..

by the way, sorry for the lack of interface.. we're just keeping
with that trend we started off with =) we may have some interface
disks coming up in the next half-a-year or so, thanks to the offers
of help from legend design and imphobia members willing to help us
out on the coding and graphics end of it.

additionally, our homepage.. http://www.fm.org/ has been someone
neglected over the past several months but we plan to get it up
to date VERY soon. meanwhile you can get all of our releases
on ftp.cdrom.com .. use the search engine on http://www.hornet.org/
and you will be able to find them all there.

regards to everyone out there...
