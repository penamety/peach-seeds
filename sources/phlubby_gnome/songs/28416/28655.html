Polyurethane audio-breeder

This one was written during a particularly shit time of my life: in early 2010 we ran out of funding for our project we were working on, so during those few months we had no money, I was sleeping on the floor, etc., and everything sucked a lot. During this time I was talking a lot to Decipher and Ferris from YUP about making a 64k so I started building a track, and I asked them if they were interested in something really aggressive. I sat down working on this track and essentially channeled all the rage and anger I had built up at the time into this, but at the same time I wanted to make sure the track is the highest possible production quality, so that people cannot fault it. In essence, I wanted to build the musical equivalent of a tactical nuclear strike - huge payload, surgical precision. The 64k itself never got made (I never saw a single pixel of it), but well, if you were at that last Breakpoint, you might remember this part of the compo.
