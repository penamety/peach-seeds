Additional Information:

Codasm : « I Really lOve this tune because of the feeling it gives, Well done, CJ ! »

During i went to the cinema watching to Roméo + Juliette with T-Bob, Doudou and ;QRIE, my bros Pype listened in loop to my new flying track…

With this Space is my Reality i started new melodic trance style inspirated from my mentor Pierrick Hansen and his Space Project, witch the 2nd essai’s samples helped me a lot !

Strangely, with a confirmated potential, i forgot to add the song to my next CD Album « Summer Island » released in summer 97. Space is my Reality only appeared in Esperenza in january 98.

Some years later, in 2002, working on the project Pixelization, i decided to rework it as a gift to my brother who likes so much that theme !
