Additional Information:

The song “Artificial Intelligence Bomb” was released as an entry for the 1st user-generated online NSF (NES Sound Format) competition Famicompo which was held from May to July 2003[1], and was written by a Japanese competitor Naruto.

The song ranked the 2nd place in the contest, and it was the highest rank among the original songs.
