Just a small funky/jazzy song that comes from my 2013 album "Microsongs" that can be listened from here: http://cheapbeatsmusic.bandcamp.com/album/microsongs
I made it for ZX Spectrum 16K with AY-interface but of course 16K is too small ram for a reasonable tracker, so the song has been composed on ZX Spectrum 48K + AY-interface (ZX SoundTracker 1.1).
Recorded from real hardware.

PS: "Ai" means "Love" in Japanese. :) The album has been released by Japan-based label CheapBeats (Tokyo).
