Sadly no.

10 out of 12 samples are ST-01. I explain this in the sample info. There is a "minor cheat" and a "major cheat" sample (a minor and major chord).

I wanted to add atmosphere to the song by using chord samples, but had only access to ST-01 and my own samples at BFP where this song was composed. I know there are chord samples in some early ST-disks, but I decided to go with my own this time.

I also mixed two st-01 samples into one (bdrum+snare). This is a feature in Protracker 2.3d so I decided that was ok since I was already cheating with the chords.

ST-01:NICE is also edited. I wasted 99% of the sample and made it a looping chip sample. This could probably also be considered a cheat.

It would have been nice to say it's an ST-01-only song, but since it isn't, I wont.

I hope you can still enjoy the oldschool vibe of it!
