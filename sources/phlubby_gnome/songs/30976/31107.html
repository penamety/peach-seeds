Ok, I figured it out (:

This IS very much like Zyx's tune, but it's the _chip_ version; and I believe it was featured at one point in one of the $keptic "Chipmania" packs. (Flite/$keptic put those together, they rule.) Which brought me to looking for Reverse/Digital's great 'Harmo-Nique' which -- well an earlier version, or different version, was on Nectarine back in the day, 2000/2001/2002, and Spectral and Nosfera2 and I used to discuss whether or not it was from an Yngve Malmsteen track, I think I thought it was and Spectral was sure it wasn't and Nosfera2 was basically ambivalent (:

So yes: this is a CHIP (15k) version of the tune from Hypnautic Hammer II. Which is similar, but different, from the Half-Brains team's track "Thow-Hypnautique" from the Sunstone demo.

I'll see if I can find the Chipmania that this is from, if it's indeed in a Chipmania pack.
