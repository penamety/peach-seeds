Additional Information:

At Dreamhack Winter 2004 there was a surprise compo (called the fast music compo) in which one had to write a piece of music using only the default set of sound samples shipped with Windows.

Lft's contribution was a cover of the song of the Humming Birds in the Donald Duck film Bird lover's expedition, traditionally broadcasted in Sweden on Christmas eve.

...and he won !
