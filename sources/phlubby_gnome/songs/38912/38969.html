I like scene music. How 'bout you?

I have to be honest. I didn't even recognize this as one of RushJet1's tunes due to the absolute lack of extra sound channels or vibrato overdose. I only just realized he was the one who did it after hearing it here on Nectarine Radio. And quite frankly... I'm pleasantly surprised. I honestly didn't know RushJet1 could make a song using the standard NES sound channels and thought he put vibrato in everything he made. I'm very happy to know I was wrong.

That aside, this is a very kickin' version of the classic "Moon" theme from the NES DuckTales. It starts out normal enough, but soon, it picks up into a high-energy remix of the track we know and love. It also makes extensive use of the Konami "sound set", heard in almost all Konami games, hence the name. Very much worth a listen!
