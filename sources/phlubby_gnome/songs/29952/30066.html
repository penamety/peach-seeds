I really liked the full mix of "Accidental Ray of Sun". I also really like playing around with Steinberg's "The Grand". I realized the song could be adapted to a piano only tune without much complication -- so I arranged it for the VSTi.

Turned out really nice.

Due to some flakiness in "The Grand" I split the piano up between two different VSTi so it would have enough polyphony for the entire song.
