Additional Information:

As you may guess, this tune was made as a joke.
I composed this one for a game on PowerTV console,
it was a 8 channel chiptune with 8 bit samples.
Later, I brought back original 16 bit samples
and decide to release it on 'Toy SongS 3' musicdisk.
I found myself singing silly melody during listening
to this tune, so I quickly plugged a microphone and
sampled my own voice. What is most funny, the vocal
pretending to be quite serious, while music has
awful chip samples. It's so easy to make a bad sound,
but nowdays it's even more easy to make a good sound :)
So, I processed vocal and left the rest as it was -
who care?.. And of course, it's not a "ragga" style :)
