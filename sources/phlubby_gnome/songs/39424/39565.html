Additional Information:

This tune is by Markus Schneider but has for quite some time appeared incorrectly in HVSC under Thomas Detert.

The remix excludes the first part of the original SID and instead concentrates on the main theme.

The human beat box was done by yours truly to add a fresh element to the remix.
