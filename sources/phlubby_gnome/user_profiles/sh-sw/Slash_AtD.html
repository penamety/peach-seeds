Public information

So, some info about me and about my creative work:

my name is Podolskiy Dennis Viktorovich aka Slash/AtD. (or Slash_AtD)
I was born in 1977 (September,12) in wonderfull city - Ryazan in Russia.

In 1992 I started my activity on Zx-Spectrum platform, I organized “Atomic Destruction Group” (ADG), and started to write music for demos and intros.
Approximately in 1994 I began to work on Amiga platform in Pro-tracker.

Then in 1995 I went to army. In army I worked on 486Dx in FT2 without active sound system, but with BEEPER!!!!, and wrote my modules.
Approximately in 1997 I changed a title of my group to “Atomic Destruction music studio” (AtD).

In 1999 I come from the army and have continued write some music for demos, intros, games and etc :).

and I continue to do it on the present days o_0 :-)))))
