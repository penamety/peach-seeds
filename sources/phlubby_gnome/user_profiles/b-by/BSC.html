Public information

I used to make music for the Amstrad CPC series using my own tracker, called Soundtrakker (see http://www.cpcwiki.eu/index.php/Soundtrakker).
Most of the tunes I made were conversions from the C64 or Amiga, some from the Spectrum, some are CPC game tune covers and finally a few original compositions.
