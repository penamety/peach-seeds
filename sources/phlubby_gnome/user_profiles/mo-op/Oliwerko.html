Ok, what about me?

I found out about demoscene and stuff a few years ago, and it was what made me begin composing. I do all kinds of music now, my specialty probably being 80s synthpop. I work in Renoise, which I really love, and some of my top demoscene favorites are Reptile/Sky, #Deathjester, Ghidorah, and then some C64 stuff. Still, nothing beats the 80s for me
