Public information

Author of Arachno SoundFont (http://www.arachnosoft.com/main/soundfont.php), a 128-instrument General MIDI (GM) sound bank for SoundFont synthesizers (Sound Blaster Live!/Audigy/X-Fi, SynthFont, VirtualMIDISynth, BASSMIDI Driver, sfz...) aimed at enhancing MIDI music.

I've been composing music since 2001.
My current tracker is ModPlug Tracker / OpenMPT.

I'm a huge fan of some demoscene artists like Karsten Koch, Skaven (Peter Hajba), Jogeir Liljedahl, Rapture (Fabian Del Priore) and many others.
